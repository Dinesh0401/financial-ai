HOW TO DEMO FOR VIVA
=================================================

1. Open chat page -> see the happy animated Zova avatar
2. Type "Hi" -> warm personalized greeting (no robotic report)
3. Click "Where am I spending the most this month?" -> top categories list
4. Click "How can I reduce my biggest expense?" -> India-specific tips with
   rupee savings + 12-month compound total
5. Click "How do I pay off my highest-interest loan faster?" -> avalanche plan
   with ETA date per loan
6. Ask a risky question like "Am I at risk of default?" -> Zova's face turns
   concerned (amber glow, flat mouth, head shake) + "Heads up" badge
7. Ask a positive question like "Am I on track?" -> face turns happy
   (wider smile, squinted eyes, sparkles) + "Good news" badge
